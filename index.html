Antikythera Mechanism

An interactive in-browser realisation of the Antikythera mechanism, driven by antikythera-spectral running in Pyodide. Five live viewports, ten research panels, ~10,000 years of fine-grained navigation, and no kernel data required — the engine is pure modular arithmetic over the mechanism's encoded period relations.

antikythera-spectral cold

    Loads Pyodide and pip-installs antikythera-spectral in your browser. First visit may take ~10–30 s; subsequent visits are cached. The mechanism's modular arithmetic — Metonic 235/19, Saros 223/19, etc. — runs entirely client-side. README on GitHub.
